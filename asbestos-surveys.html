Asbestos Surveys

Whatever your needs, be it asbestos management surveys, or repair / demolition surveys, we can find the level, type, and surface treatment of every asbestos on your property and make a detailed report on your asbestos problems.

If you are the assignment holder for commercial property, you can be sued if you fail to comply with the new asbestos regulations.

Asbestos containing materials (ACMs) are still present throughout properties in the UK. Our asbestos surveys will enable you to know what ACMs are present in your building and what you need to do to create an effective asbestos management plan.

Management Survey
Following the release of HSG264 by the HSE, Asbestos Management Surveys have replaced what used to be known as Type 1 and Type 2 Asbestos Surveys. The Asbestos Management Survey now requires sampling to be undertaken to determine all asbestos materials that are readily accessible.

Our Asbestos Management Survey will assess what asbestos is present in areas that can be accessed without intrusively damaging the property. This is aimed at determining the presence of asbestos in communal areas, industrial properties, offices, rental properties, and for buyers and sellers of a property.

Refurbishment and Demolition Survey
Likewise with the Renovation and Demolition survey, after the release of HSG264, the Asbestos Type 3 Survey was replaced by the Asbestos Demolition / Renovation Survey. The Asbestos Renovation and Destruction Survey is an investigation of the core of a building and requires access to each area. It sets out to determine the location, area and condition of asbestos throughout the property so that asbestos risk assessments can be carried out.

If necessary, asbestos samples will be taken to test whether there is asbestos and what type - Chrysotile (white asbestos), Amosite (Brown asbestos), or Crocidolite (Blue asbestos).

This asbestos survey will highlight what materials need to be removed and how to manage asbestos before work begins so that builders or demolition contractors working on properties containing asbestos can do it safely.

Based in UK, our asbestos specialist will remove and dispose of asbestos throughout Europe.
