Airs Asbestos – Committed to Health and Safety

Airs Asbestos Removal Ltd is licensed by the Health & Safety Executive (HSE) to undertake works with asbestos containing materials and is fully insured to undertake such works.

Our management and employees have received all necessary regulatory training, medicals and other testing to undertake such works and are constantly reviewed on site providing information to allow a detailed training needs analysis for each employee to identify where further training opportunities exist.

Random Personal and boundary air monitoring of our site operatives along with unannounced internal and external site audits are standard procedure on all our sites. Results are recorded and investigated in order to ensure we are employing latest industry best practice and are fully compliant with current and changing legislation. All employees are required to attend, and contribute to, our Health & Safety Committee meetings where all matters relating to asbestos specific along with more general health and safety findings and issues are considered.

WHERE IS ASBESTOS?

Asbestos can be found in any building constructed or refurbished before the year 2000. It affects both industrial and residential properties and can be found in some surrising places.

Asbestos is hazardous and it can be found in many of the common materials that were used in the building trade up until the year 2000.

Asbestos is no longer used in construction or refurbishment of any buildings but many thousands of tonnes of it were used in the past and much of it is still in place. As long as it is in good condition and is not being disturbed, damaged or exposed there is no imminent risk, but it is important you know where it might be for any future works that may be undertaken.
